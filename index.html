Acer Books · founded by Tao Zhijian, PhD

Translator, scholar, and member of the Chinese Writers Association in Quebec. After publishing the acclaimed The Waste Land / The Love Song and English Poems in Chinese Poetic Forms, Tao launched Acer Books to bridge cultures through finely crafted editions. Every cover reflects an artist's eye — many photographs you see here are his own.
